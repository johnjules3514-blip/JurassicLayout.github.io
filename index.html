My Artworks

Nigersaurus

I recently completed an artwork of the Nigersaurus, a fascinating sauropod known for its unique wide mouth and hundreds of tiny teeth. In this piece, I aimed to highlight both its gentle presence and its unusual features, blending scientific accuracy with artistic expression. Through color, texture, and composition, I wanted to bring this often-overlooked dinosaur to life and showcase its place in the prehistoric world.

Tyrannosaurus rex

I finished an artwork of the Tyrannosaurus rex, one of the most iconic and powerful dinosaurs in history. My goal was to capture its strength and presence while adding my own creative interpretation. Through details in texture, posture, and atmosphere, I wanted to show why the T. rex continues to inspire awe as the king of the dinosaurs.
